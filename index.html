Can AI Master the Royal Game?
An Interactive Exploration of Chess Position Analysis by Modern AI

🎯 Research Question & Theme

Question: How well can modern AI language models analyze chess positions and identify the best moves?

Why This Topic?

Chess represents the perfect intersection of pattern recognition, strategic thinking, and tactical calculation. Unlike simple object recognition, chess analysis requires understanding complex positional relationships, calculating multiple move sequences, and identifying tactical motifs. This makes it an ideal testbed for evaluating AI's visual and analytical capabilities.

What Makes This Fascinating:

  • Chess positions contain rich visual and logical information
  • There are objectively correct answers (unlike subjective image analysis)
  • Mistakes reveal specific gaps in AI reasoning
  • Tests both pattern recognition AND logical analysis
